## 3. Our Trading Methodology

We execute using Smart Money Concepts (SMC) and ICT principles. 

### The AMD Cycle
Our setups are primarily based on the Accumulation, Manipulation, Distribution (AMD) cycle across major trading sessions:
1.  **Accumulation (Asia Session):** The market builds a range and engineers liquidity on both sides.
2.  **Manipulation (London Open / NY Open):** The market sweeps the engineered liquidity (stop hunt).
3.  **Distribution (London Continuation / NY Session):** The market reveals its true direction and expands toward the opposing liquidity pool.

### Core Setups
We execute when we see:
-   Liquidity sweeps into higher timeframe (HTF) Point of Interest (POI) or Order Blocks (OB).
-   Breaks of Structure (BOS) or Changes of Character (CHoCH) confirming a shift in momentum.
-   Fair Value Gaps (FVG) acting as entry triggers or targets.

### Primary Assets
-   **Indices/Crypto:** BTC/USD
-   **Metals:** XAU/USD (Gold), XAG/USD (Silver)
-   **Forex Majors:** EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY

## 4. Signal Execution Rules (For Members)

When a signal is posted in the VIP Group, members are expected to follow these strict rules to ensure capital preservation:

1.  **Risk Management is Non-Negotiable:** Never risk more than 1% to 2% of total account equity on a single setup.
2.  **Use Limit Orders:** If the price is near the entry, use a limit order to ensure precision. Do not market buy if the price has already run away from the entry zone.
3.  **Follow Trade Updates:** If an update says "SL to Entry" or "Take 50% partial," execute it immediately. We protect capital first.
4.  **No Emotional Hedging:** If a trade hits Stop Loss (SL), we accept the loss. We do not double down. We wait for the next setup.
