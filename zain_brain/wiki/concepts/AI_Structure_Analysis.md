# AI Structure Analysis

## Thesis
AI-driven structure analysis provides traders with an advanced tool to identify market structures such as Break of Structure (BOS), Change of Character (CHOCH), and Fair Value Gaps (FVG), enhancing decision-making and trade execution.

## Structural Rules
- **Upload Chart for Analysis:** Use the Zen Vision Engine to upload TradingView screenshots for instant analysis.
- **Identify Key Structures:** AI identifies BOS, CHOCH, and FVG, providing a probabilistic alpha bias for trade setups.
- **Integrate with Trading Plan:** Use AI insights to refine and validate existing trade plans, ensuring alignment with institutional strategies.

## Edge Cases
- **AI Misinterpretation:** Cross-verify AI-generated structures with manual analysis to avoid reliance on potential AI errors.
- **Market Volatility:** During high volatility periods, AI analysis may lag or misinterpret rapid market changes.
