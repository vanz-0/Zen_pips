# News Manipulation and Trading Strategies

## Thesis
News manipulation is a tactic used by institutions to create liquidity and trap retail traders. Understanding this can help traders anticipate market moves and align with institutional strategies.

## Structural Rules
- **Monitor News Releases:** Check economic calendars like Forex Factory for high-impact news events that could affect market liquidity.
- **Identify Institutional Traps:** Look for price movements that contradict news sentiment, indicating potential traps set by institutions.
- **Adjust Trading Strategy:** Use news events to time entries and exits, capitalizing on the volatility and liquidity created by institutional manipulation.

## Edge Cases
- **Unexpected News Impact:** Be prepared for unexpected market reactions to news, which may not align with historical patterns.
- **Delayed Reactions:** Sometimes, the market may react to news with a delay. Monitor price action closely post-announcement.
