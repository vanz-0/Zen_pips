## 🎯 Feature Breakdown

### 1. Live Trading Terminal
Real-time market data for XAU/USD and BTC/USD displayed on the homepage. Active signals show Entry, SL, TP1-TP3 levels with live P&L tracking. All data is pulled live from our verified database — nothing is fabricated.

### 2. Signal Delivery System
Every signal goes through a **3-step automated pipeline**:
1. **Screenshot Analysis** — TradingView chart is processed by AI Vision to extract Entry, SL, and TP levels
2. **Database Insertion** — Signal is logged to our Supabase `signals` table with full metadata
3. **Instant Broadcast** — Signal is simultaneously pushed to VIP Telegram + live website terminal

**Result:** Zero delay between analysis → publication. Every signal is timestamped and verifiable.

### 3. Trading Journal (Members Only)
A built-in journaling system connected directly to live signals:
- Log trades with pair, direction, entry/exit, and outcome
- **Signals Taken/Missed Analysis** — Automatically cross-references your entries against posted signals
- Stats dashboard: Win rate, total pips, current streak, most common mistakes
- Full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) with cloud sync

### 4. Education Vault (Members Only)
A curated library of **63 verified PDF resources** covering:
- ICT/SMC methodology (Order Blocks, FVGs, Liquidity Sweeps)
- Market Structure analysis
- Candlestick patterns and price action
- Risk management and trading psychology
- Session-specific trading strategies (London, New York)

Resources are filtered by category and difficulty level (Foundation → Intermediate → Elite).

### 5. Institutional AI Chatbot
An AI assistant trained on our entire educational library using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):
- Ask any trading question and get answers grounded in our verified PDFs
- Understands ICT/SMC terminology, Zen Pips methodology, and operational procedures
- Intelligent routing: Trading questions pull from the education database; operational questions pull from SOPs

### 6. Member Profile & Dashboard
Authenticated members get a unified workspace:
- **Profile** — Trading stats, Telegram link, account management
- **Journal** — Trade logging and performance analytics
- **Vault** — Educational resource downloads
- All accessible from a single dashboard without page reloads
