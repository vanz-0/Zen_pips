# USD Pairs — Institutional Logic

## Core Pairs
- EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, USD/CHF, USD/CAD, AUD/USD, NZD/USD

## The Dollar Index (DXY) Principle
- USD is the **base currency of global liquidity**. When DXY rises, ALL USD counter-pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD) fall.
- Always check DXY bias on H4/Daily BEFORE trading any USD pair.
- If DXY is sweeping sell-side liquidity → expect USD weakness → EUR/USD and GBP/USD rally.

## Session Timing
- **London Session (08:00-12:00 UTC)**: Initial manipulation phase. Price often fakes one direction before NY confirms the real move.
- **New York Session (13:00-17:00 UTC)**: The true institutional delivery window. Highest volume, largest displacements.
- **London/NY Overlap (13:00-16:00 UTC)**: THE KILLZONE. This is where 70%+ of daily range is delivered. Primary trading window.

## Common USD Manipulation Patterns
- **NFP Friday**: Expect a massive spike in one direction (liquidity sweep) followed by a full reversal within 30 minutes.
- **FOMC Days**: Consolidation all day until the announcement, then violent displacement. Wait 15+ minutes before entering.
- **US CPI**: Similar to NFP but typically resolves faster. The first 5 minutes are pure noise.
