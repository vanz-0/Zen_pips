# Stocks & Indices — Institutional Logic

## Core Indices
- S&P 500 (US500), NASDAQ 100 (NAS100), Dow Jones (US30)
- DAX 40 (GER40), FTSE 100 (UK100)

## Opening Range Gap Strategy
- US equity indices open at 14:30 UTC (NYSE open).
- If price gaps UP from previous close → institutions often sell into the gap, filling it within the first 30-60 minutes.
- If price gaps DOWN → institutions buy the gap fill. "Gap fills" succeed ~70% of the time on S&P 500.
- Only trade gap fills when the gap is less than 0.5%. Larger gaps may be "breakaway gaps" that don't fill.

## CME Futures Close Gaps
- Similar to Crypto CME gaps, equity futures close at 21:00 UTC Friday and reopen Sunday 23:00 UTC.
- Gap between Friday close and Sunday open tends to fill within the week.

## Earnings Season Volatility
- During earnings season (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct), individual stocks see 5-15% moves on earnings reports.
- Indices become choppier. Avoid index trading 30 minutes before and after major companies report (AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, AMZN).

## Correlation with Forex
- Strong USD (DXY rising) → US equities tend to weaken (capital flows to bonds).
- Weak USD → Equities tend to rally (cheaper dollar = more corporate earnings).
- Risk-on sentiment: Equities UP, Gold DOWN, JPY pairs UP, AUD/USD UP.
- Risk-off sentiment: Equities DOWN, Gold UP, JPY pairs DOWN, USD/CHF DOWN.
