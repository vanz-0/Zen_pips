# SMC Chart Analysis Protocol

## Purpose
This concept defines how the Chat AI and Chart AI systems analyze uploaded chart screenshots using Smart Money Concepts (SMC) and Inner Circle Trader (ICT) methodology.

## What To Identify On Any Chart

### 1. Market Structure
- **BOS (Break of Structure)**: Price breaks a previous swing high (bullish) or swing low (bearish), confirming trend continuation.
- **CHoCH (Change of Character)**: The first break against the current trend, signaling potential reversal. This is the earliest signal of a shift.
- **Higher Timeframe Bias**: Always check H4/Daily for the macro direction before analyzing lower timeframes (M15/M5).

### 2. Liquidity Zones
- **Buy-Side Liquidity (BSL)**: Clusters of retail stop-losses resting above equal highs, swing highs, and resistance levels. Smart money drives price UP to trigger these before selling.
- **Sell-Side Liquidity (SSL)**: Clusters below equal lows, swing lows, and support levels. Smart money drives price DOWN to trigger these before buying.
- **Inducement**: A smaller liquidity grab within a larger one—retail traders enter early, get stopped out, then the real move begins.

### 3. Order Blocks (OB)
- The **last bearish candle before a bullish move** (bullish OB) or the **last bullish candle before a bearish move** (bearish OB).
- Must be validated by a displacement (strong momentum candle leaving the zone).
- Only trade OBs that sit in premium/discount zones.

### 4. Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
- A three-candle pattern where the wick of candle 1 and candle 3 do not overlap, leaving an imbalance.
- Price tends to return to fill this gap. Use FVGs as entry refinement zones.
- Inversion FVG: When price fills the FVG and the gap becomes support/resistance.

### 5. Premium & Discount Zones
- Use Fibonacci 0.5 level from the most recent swing.
- **Premium Zone** (above 0.5): Ideal for SELL entries.
- **Discount Zone** (below 0.5): Ideal for BUY entries.
- Never buy in premium or sell in discount.

## Confluence Minimum
Every signal or chart analysis MUST have at least 3 of the following:
- ✅ Market Structure alignment (H4/H1 direction)
- ✅ OB or Breaker Block identification
- ✅ Premium/Discount zone confirmation
- ✅ Liquidity sweep completed
- ✅ Session timing (London/NY Killzone)
