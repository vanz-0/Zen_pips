# Gold (XAU/USD) — Institutional Logic

## Characteristics
- XAU/USD is the **most liquid commodity pair**. Daily ranges of 200-400 pips ($20-$40) are standard.
- pip_multiplier = 10 (1 pip = $0.10 movement, e.g., 2380.00 → 2380.10).
- Strongly correlated with USD inversely and with risk sentiment.

## Session Behavior
- **Asian Session**: Low volatility consolidation. Price often builds the day's liquidity pool.
- **London Open**: Gold wakes up violently. Expect Asian session highs/lows to be swept within the first hour.
- **NY Session**: The institutional delivery. Gold's largest moves happen during NY, especially around US data releases.

## Key Manipulation Patterns
- **The London Fake-Out**: Gold sweeps one side of Asian range during London, then reverses hard into NY.
- **NFP Gold**: Gold is extremely reactive to NFP. Expect 300+ pip moves within 30 minutes. NEVER hold gold through NFP without a 400+ pip stop-loss.
- **Safe Haven Flows**: During geopolitical crises, gold moves independently of USD. Monitor VIX and bond yields.

## Confluence Protocol for Gold
- Minimum 3 confluences required (same as all pairs).
- Gold Order Blocks are valid on H1/H4 only — M15 OBs on gold are unreliable due to extreme volatility.
- Always check the 200 EMA on H4 as dynamic support/resistance.
