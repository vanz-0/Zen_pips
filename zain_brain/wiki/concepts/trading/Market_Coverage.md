# ZAIN Market Coverage: The Institutional Horizon

This index defines the official trading instruments tracked and analyzed by the Zen Pips ecosystem. All AI agents, chart analyzers, and signal engines align with this catalog.

---

## 💱 1. Forex Markets
### Major Pairs & Crosses
- **Euro Markets**: EUR/USD (Primary), EUR/GBP, EUR/JPY, EUR/CAD, EUR/AUD, EUR/CHF, EUR/NZD.
- **GBP Markets**: GBP/USD (Cable), GBP/JPY (The Dragon), GBP/AUD, GBP/CAD, GBP/CHF, GBP/NZD.
- **USD Majors**: USD/JPY, USD/CAD, USD/CHF, NZD/USD, AUD/USD.
- **Minor Features**: AUD/JPY, CAD/JPY, CHF/JPY, AUD/CAD.

## 🏆 2. Commodities (Metals)
- **Gold (XAU/USD)**: Primary institutional asset for liquidity and volatility analysis.
- **Silver (XAG/USD)**: Secondary metal, closely correlated with Gold but with higher volatility percentage.

## 📈 3. Global Indices
- **US Tech 100 (NAS100 / NASDAQ)**: High-speed technological displacement analysis.
- **US Wall St 30 (US30 / Dow Jones)**: Industrial institutional trend tracking.
- **US SPX 500 (US500 / S&P)**: The benchmark for global market sentiment.
- **UK 100 (FTSE100)**: Primary European institutional index.

## ₿ 4. Crypto Assets
### Stablecoins
- **USDT (Tether)**: Primary denomination for crypto logic.
- **USDC / BUSD**: Secondary monitors for stablecoin peg stability.

### Active Coins
- **Bitcoin (BTC/USD)**: The market leader for CME gap and weekend manipulation logic.
- **Ethereum (ETH/USD)**: Altcoin market sentiment lead.
- **Altcoin Features**: Solana (SOL), Ripple (XRP), Tron (TRX), Dogecoin (DOGE).

## 🏢 5. Share CFDs (Equity Displacement)
Tracking the "Big Names" that drive market liquidity:
- **Nvidia (NVDA)**: AI-driven market volatility lead.
- **Tesla (TSLA)**: Retail vs. Institutional sentiment barometer.
- **Apple (AAPL)**, **Amazon (AMZN)**, **Alphabet (GOOGL)**, **Microsoft (MSFT)**.

## 🚫 Excluded Markets (Low Institutional Priority)
- **Bonds**: Not currently traded in the Zen Pips strategy.
- **Synthetic Indices**: Excluded to maintain institutional integrity based on real-world market liquidity.
